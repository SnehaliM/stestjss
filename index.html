SEASONS

Summer

Sometimes, rain comes along with lightning and thunder. The Rainy Season comes after the Summer Season, so, this season brings relief to the burning earth scorched in the summer heat. The temperature remains pleasant during the monsoon. All the living things enjoy the rainy season by getting wet into the rain water.
